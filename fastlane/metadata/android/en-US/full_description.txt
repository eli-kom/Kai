Need quick AI answers without the bloat? Kai is your lightning-fast, open-source companion, powered by models like Llama and Google's Gemini. Dive into seamless chats for brainstorming, writing, summarizing, learning, or casual talks – all in a minimalist interface that loads in seconds.

Supported AI Services
• Ollama: Connect to your self-hosted local AI models for privacy-focused chats
• Gemini: Google's advanced AI with built-in file attachment support
• xAI: Access innovative Grok models for creative and insightful responses
• Groq: Experience ultra-fast AI inference for quick replies
• Mistral AI: Utilize the free tier for cost-effective AI interactions

Key Features
• Encrypted Local Storage: Securely store your chat history on your device with end-to-end encryption
• Text-to-Speech: Hear AI responses aloud with natural-sounding voice output
• File Attachments: Easily upload files to enhance your conversations (supported on Gemini)
• Seamless Service Switching: Switch between AI providers effortlessly mid-conversation
• Multi-Turn Conversations: Enjoy context-aware chats where AI remembers previous messages
• Simple Chat Interface: User-friendly design for beginners and experts alike

AI Tools (Enable in Settings)
• Get Local Time: AI provides accurate current date and time info
• Get Location: Receive location-based responses using IP detection
• Send Notification: Get push notifications triggered by AI
• Create Calendar Event: Add events to your calendar straight from the chat

Privacy First
Kai prioritizes your privacy: All chat history is stored locally with strong encryption. When using cloud AI services, your data is sent directly to the providers for processing – no data collection by the app itself. Stay in control with this open-source AI chat app.
